Dataset Overview



Understanding the Dataset

Our comprehensive Alzheimer dataset is curated from multiple medical institutions and verified by expert radiologists. Each image is carefully annotated to ensure high-quality data for research and development.

Image Characteristics

Technical Specifications

- Resolution: 256 x 256 pixels
- Formats: DICOM and NIfTI
- Sequences: T1, T2, and FLAIR
- Annotations: Tumor location and segmentation masks
- Quality: Medical-grade imaging
